Why Lookin

Your face is your password.

Lookin replaces forgettable passwords with face authentication. Sign up once, enroll your face, and log in with a glance — no codes to remember, no resets to chase.

Every face login runs a liveness check, so a photo or a screen can't impersonate you. Your face data is stored as a one-way embedding, never as an image.
